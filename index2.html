I've always been drawn to the overlap between concept and design. Utilizing Adobe Creative Suite to develop out-of-this-world templates, logos and more and bringing them to life through HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

From creating Howrse templates in my early adolescence to attempting to hack my own computer, my passion for computer programming has blossomed. I have since improved my abilities in both the industry and from my education from Robertson College.

What began as a hobby in my teens, is now a full-time passion. I have been influenced by freelancing and have developed conceptual art and design skills, through education at Visual College of Art and Design.
